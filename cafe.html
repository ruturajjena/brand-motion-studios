MASTER PROMPT — Build BR Velluto (Luxury Italian Cafe Experience)

You are an award-winning frontend engineer, interaction designer and creative developer.

Your task is to build a world-class scroll storytelling website for an Italian café called BR Velluto.

The website should feel like a mix of
* Apple
* Awwwards Site of the Day
* Minimal Gallery
* Porsche configurator
* Dior

Final Goal

The final experience should feel like a premium interactive brand film, not a conventional restaurant website. Every scroll should reveal a new chapter: the visitor approaches BR Velluto, enters the café, explores the atmosphere, watches the signature pizza come to life, dives into the artistry of fresh pasta, and finishes with an elegant dessert experience. The site should be polished enough to feel at home on Awwwards, with smooth cinematic transitions, editorial typography, subtle micro-interactions, and flawless performance across desktop, tablet, and mobile.
